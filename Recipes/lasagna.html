Cheesy Lasagna

Lasagna! The classic italian dish and dinnertime staple. Lasagna is a dish that every home has come to love and cherish, and now you too can enjoy a restaurant quality dish of lasagna right at home!

Ingrediants

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings:Parsley, basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese:Parmesan, mozzarella, and riccota
  • Egg
