Chewy Choclate Chip Cookies!

Chocolate chip cookies are a baking staple! Everyone knows them, everyone loves them, and now, you'll know how to make them!

A family recipe made by my grandma, these cookies are soft and chewy on the inside, while still being crispy on the outside. The melty chocolate chips and warm cookie pairs perfectly with a nice glass of cold milk!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Optional:

  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Diced Tomatos
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado/Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Cook Ground beef in a pan until browned, and then add taco seasoning.
  2. While beef is browning, put refried beans in a pot and heat until creamy.
  3. Once beef is down browning anf beans are ready, apply a small layer of beans in the hardshell.
  4. Add a layer of beef on top of the beans.
  5. Add optional toppings to your liking, such as cheese, lettuce, sour cream etc.
  6. Enjoy your hard earned tacos!
